# Release — July 1, 2026

**Version:** `c06a6bc`
**Environment:** Production (courtops.app)

---

## Time Clock, Schedule & Availability — Quality-of-Life Batch

### Improvements

- **Full clock history.** The Recent Clock History list previously showed only the last 7 days. It now shows the last 30 days by default, and admins get a date-range picker in the section header to pull up any older period on demand — no more "I can't see entries before a certain date."

- **Stale swap posts cleaned up.** Open swap and take requests whose shift date has already passed no longer clutter the Shift Swap board. Past posts are hidden automatically (they'll reappear in the upcoming swap history view).

- **One way to release a schedule.** Build mode previously had two overlapping actions: "Publish drafts" (immediate, no review) and "Release" (with coverage review). The bulk Publish button is gone — releasing a window, with its coverage summary and gap warnings, is now the single path from draft to live. Individual shifts can still be published one-off from the shift detail popover.

- **Availability due dates you can't miss.** Window due dates are now urgency-colored badges with days remaining (red = due within a day, orange = within 3 days), and the availability calendar shows a banner for your most urgent unsubmitted window — it stays until you submit.

- **Clearer calendar navigation.** The back/Today/forward controls and the month label across all calendar views are larger and higher-contrast.

### Under the hood

- New CourtOps brand assets and a formalized design-token palette (no visual changes — groundwork for consistent styling and future per-club branding).
- Payroll time-clock export design finalized; question list sent to the payroll provider (ATY) to confirm the Phoenix import format.
