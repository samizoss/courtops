## Schedule Builder — Bug Fixes & UX Improvements

### Bug Fixes

- **Instructor & League Leader roles now work in build mode.** Previously these two roles failed with "Failed to update shift" due to a database constraint that only allowed 4 of the 6 roles. All six roles (Front Desk, Coaching, Instructor, League Leader, Management, Other) now save correctly.

### Improvements

- **15-minute scheduling increments.** Drag-to-assign in the schedule grid now snaps to 15-minute blocks instead of 30-minute. Gives more flexibility for shorter shifts and non-standard start times.

- **My-shift highlighting.** Your own published shifts now show an orange ring in the schedule view, making them easy to spot at a glance without reading names. Works in both "My schedule" and "Total schedule" views.

- **Drag-to-assign over existing shifts.** You can now drag to create a new shift directly over a time slot that already has someone assigned. Previously you had to find an empty area and adjust times manually.

- **Improved date readability.** Date numbers in the schedule header are larger and bolder. The first visible day and the 1st of each month show a three-letter month abbreviation (e.g., "Jun 4"). Day column dividers are more pronounced.

- **Touch device stability.** Added pointer cancel handling to prevent the drag overlay from getting stuck on tablets and phones.

### Database

- Migration `fix_shifts_role_check` applied — expanded the `shifts.role` CHECK constraint to include `instructor` and `league-leader`.
