## Coverage-First Magic Schedule + Draft Management

- **Rewritten magic schedule algorithm.** Coverage-first greedy approach: builds a 30-min slot grid, identifies coverage gaps, fills them with the best-fit available staff (sorted by furthest below target hours). Produces reasonable shift lengths instead of full-availability-window blocks.
- **Draft management UX.** "Clear magic drafts" and "Clear all drafts" bulk buttons. Magic drafts show purple dashed borders with "magic" label. Manual drafts show orange dashed borders.
- **Release flow.** ReleaseScheduleModal with summary cards (drafts/published/gaps), staff hours breakdown, daily coverage grid, and bulk publish. Green "Release" buttons per availability window in toolbar.
- **Magic schedule settings.** Min shift hours, min staff on duty, default weekly target hours — configurable in Settings > General.
- **Visual differentiation.** Magic vs manual drafts vs published shifts are visually distinct in the schedule grid.

## Schedule PDF Export
