## Monthly Newsletter Builder & Weekly Digest

### New: Newsletter Builder (Settings → admin only, `/newsletter`)

Building the monthly Court Reserve email newsletter used to mean writing every line by hand. Now:

- **Paste your notes** — any messy format works (bullets, half-sentences, copy-pasted texts) — and fill in the structured facts: hero topic + link, league rows, event rows, registration-open dates, a coach's quote, and member/staff spotlight names.
- Click **Generate** and AI writes the newsletter copy in your voice — it never invents the HTML or layout, only the words that go in each slot, so the email always looks like a CourtOps newsletter.
- A built-in checklist blocks **Copy HTML** if anything's missing: an empty slot, a fact you didn't fill in, or a link that isn't secure. Fix what it flags and generate again.
- Preview the finished newsletter right on the page, then **Copy HTML** and paste it into a Court Reserve email campaign. Links are automatically tagged for tracking.

### New: Weekly Digest — "This Week @ The Jar" (`/weekly-digest`, admin only)

A ready-to-send weekly roundup with zero writing required — it's pulled straight from your Court Reserve schedule, not written by AI.

- Every **Friday at 9am**, CourtOps automatically builds next week's (Monday–Sunday) digest from your live Court Reserve events and emails admins a link to review it. It never sends on its own — you always review and copy it yourself.
- Need it sooner, or something changed? Click **Generate now** on the Weekly Digest page any time.
- Two ready-to-use pieces come out of every digest:
  - An email-ready **"week at a glance" HTML** — Copy HTML and paste into your Court Reserve email, same as the newsletter.
  - A **downloadable social graphic** (Instagram-post-sized) you can post directly — it automatically shrinks the layout on busy weeks so nothing gets cut off.
- Note: only events with at least one Court Reserve registration can appear — that's a Court Reserve data limitation, not a CourtOps one.
- If something goes wrong (a bad Court Reserve connection, etc.), you'll get an alert email and the page falls back to showing your last successful digest instead of a blank page.

## Same-day updates (v1.1)

- **Times fixed**: digest and content-calendar times briefly showed 5–6 hours early (a server timezone bug — "LTP-Monday 6pm" showed as 1:00 PM). All times now display exactly as they appear in Court Reserve, and previously stored data was corrected.
- **Staff access**: all staff can now open Newsletter and Weekly Digest to view, copy, and download. Generating (which calls paid/external APIs) stays admin-only.
- **Links everywhere**: digest event names now link to their Court Reserve pages, and the footer links the address (Google Maps), website, Instagram, and Facebook.
- **Easier to read**: the digest's date range switched from red-on-blue (very low contrast) to white — same fix in the email and the social graphic.
- **Newsletter pre-fill from Court Reserve**: pick your month and click **Load from Court Reserve** — every event that month appears with an include/exclude checkbox (recurring ones like Open Play default to excluded, with a "runs N× this month" badge). Included events drop straight into the Upcoming Events and League Lineup sections with dates, times, and registration links already filled in. Everything stays editable, and rows you typed yourself are never overwritten.

## Same-day updates (v1.2) — newsletter section toggles

- **Section on/off toggles**: every newsletter section except the hero, "This Month at a Glance," and the sign-off now has a switch. No member spotlight this month? Turn it off and it disappears from the email — the checklist no longer blocks you over content a section you switched off would have needed. Leagues, Events, Clinics, Announcements, and Looking Ahead start on; community photo, Member Spotlight, Staff Shout-Out, and Coach's Corner start off. Toggles are kept across **Regenerate copy**.
- **Registration windows reworked**: the global "member registration open" / "daily player registration open" fields are gone (that info lives in Court Reserve and wasn't tied to a specific event). In their place: one optional **"League registration info"** line inside the Leagues section that appears in the email exactly as typed — leave it blank and the line is dropped entirely. The AI never writes it.
- **Month picker first, preload up top**: the month/year picker now sits at the very top of the form, with the Court Reserve preload directly beneath it — its button says exactly what it will load ("Load August 2026 events from Court Reserve"). Changing the month clears the stale checklist and flags previously applied Court Reserve rows so you know to reload; rows you typed or edited yourself are never touched.
