## Schedule History Fix

### Bug Fixes

- **The schedule now shows every date, not just the last week.** Previously the Schedule view only loaded shifts from 7 days back to 6 weeks ahead — navigating further back (e.g. reviewing early-June shifts for payroll) showed an empty calendar even though the shifts were safely in the system. The schedule now loads additional history automatically as you navigate, with a brief "Loading schedule…" note while it fetches. No data was ever lost — this was a display issue only.
